Restaurant Description

Fun Food, an Indian restaurant in Mumbai, Maharashtra aims to offer authentic Indian food at an affordable price. Our mission is to serve people fresh ethnic food, presenting it in a new light and developing a customer base for a quick and healthy Indian meal.

We serve Indian food with intricate flavors and introduce the aroma of freshly roasted and ground spices that are made at home with authentic traditions of India in every bite. Choose your choice of spice level and taste. Think of us as a place to get a good break after a work day and to rejuvenate for the rest of the day.
